Crepes

Crepes are traditionally a French treat usually eating for snack time in the afternoon. Children love to eat Crepes filled with chocolate, sugar, or jam. They are like a much thinner pancake, although it is rare to eat them with maple syrup.

They are very easy to make and children enjoy helping out as well. Come on and let's give it a try!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • dash of salt
  • 2 teaspoons melted butter

    • Steps

    1. Mix together the flour, eggs, and milk.
    2. Add the salt and finally the melted butter.
    3. Pour enough batter for a thin layer to cover the entire bottom of the hot pan.
    4. Flip once small bubbles appear in batter.
    5. Serve hot or refrigerate for later.
